About this course

This twelve week Creative Writing course offers essential skill sets required for professional writing for English Language Learners. In this course, learners focus on understanding the demands of different styles of writing, and learn a variety of techniques to improve their writing process and enhance creativity. The course also allows learners to improve grammatical appropriateness, vocabulary improvement, revision and editing skills.

Learners will have the opportunity to write on a number of different topics and ideas, with an opportunity to write for online publications. If their contents qualify for publication their contents will be paid and credited on the publication.

The course materials will be offered via lectures and digital formats. Learners are required to complete several projects during the course. Projects include short/ long writing assignments and journal entries.

What you'll learn

  • Build persuasive writing techniques and essay development skills
  • Learn writing formats, critical thinking, proofreading, self-editing, revision, tone, and vocabulary
  • Develop strong professional writing and communication skills
